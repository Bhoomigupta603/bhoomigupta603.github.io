What does MoodAstra do?

MoodAstra is an AI-powered movie recommender system that suggests films based on the user's emotional state. It analyzes mood inputs such as happy, sad, romantic, or energetic and recommends movies that match the user's feelings.

Why this project matters

MoodAstra shows how Artificial Intelligence can create emotionally intelligent systems that enhance user experience. Emotion-aware recommendations improve engagement and demonstrate how AI can personalize digital content in entertainment platforms.
