What does Voice TaskMate do?

Voice TaskMate is an AI-powered personal assistant that listens to human voice commands and performs tasks such as opening applications, searching the web, telling the time, and responding intelligently. It uses speech recognition and natural language processing to understand user intent.

Why this project matters

Voice TaskMate demonstrates how AI can create hands-free intelligent systems that improve accessibility and productivity. Voice-based AI is widely used in smart assistants, automation systems, and assistive technologies.
