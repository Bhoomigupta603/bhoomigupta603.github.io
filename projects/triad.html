What does Triad Binarize Defender do?

Triad Binarize Defender is an AI-powered image processing system designed to protect digital watermarks. It uses deep learning to detect watermark regions and convert them into clean, high-contrast binary images to improve security, readability, and robustness against tampering.

Why this project matters

Digital watermark security is critical for copyright protection, media authentication, and fraud prevention. This project demonstrates how modern AI can automatically detect and protect watermark information, making digital assets safer from piracy and manipulation.
